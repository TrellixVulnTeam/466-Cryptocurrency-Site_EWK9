About Us

ProjectCoin was heavily influenced by CoinBase, a website used for tracking, selling and buying cryptocurrency. ProjectCoin is created using Django in Python. Our team was made up of six students at California State University, Fullerton for the class 466 Software Process -- instructed by Lidia Morrison in Spring 2019. Our team was split off into front-end and back-end portions, while others worked on the Software Architecture Document (SAD).
